If you're having poor video quality on a DVD or Blu-ray disc you got from Netflix, use this article to resolve the issue.

Note: DVDs and the Queue feature are only available in the US.

If you're experiencing any of the following:

  • The picture is stretched out or cut off.
  • The black levels are gray or washed out.
  • Bad video quality on a Blu-ray disc.
  • A green stripe running down every copy of a specific title.

Follow the troubleshooting steps below to resolve the issue.

  1. Bypass any receivers or stereo equipment by connecting the device directly to the TV.
  2. If you are using an HDMI cable, try reversing the ends or trying a new HDMI cable.
  3. Switch from HDMI to a component or composite audio/video cable.
  4. If the picture is stretched or cut off, changing the screen resolution on the TV or device may resolve the issue.
  5. Try zooming in or out, if your TV has that option.
  6. If you've completed the steps above and are still experiencing the issue, try playing the disc in another player. This will let you know if the problem is the player or the TV.

If you still experience frequent video issues after following these steps, please contact the manufacturer of your player for further assistance.
