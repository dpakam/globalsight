General tab (600)

Use this tab to define general pump and analog data parameters for your 600 Series Controller.

Pump type

Current pump type. If the W600 is part of a configured chromatographic system, Empower software reads the pump type and displays it in this field as a read-only status. If you are adding the W600 to your instrument method using the Add Instrument tool () (when creating an instrument method without specifying a system), select the appropriate pump type from this list:

  • 600E (Default, indicates a 600 or 610)
  • 600
  • 650E
  • 610E
  • 616
  • 626
  • 625 (indicates a 625 LC or an ActION Analyzer)
  • 2000 (indicates a Delta Prep or Prep LC)
  • 3000 (indicates a Delta Prep or Prep LC)
  • 4000 (indicates a Delta Prep or Prep LC)

Head volume

Pump head volume. If the W600 is part of a configured chromatographic system, Empower software reads the pump head volume and displays it in this field as a read-only status. If you are adding the W600 to your instrument method using the Add Instrument tool () (when creating an instrument method without specifying a system), select the appropriate pump head volume from this list.

SILK enable

Smoothes solvent flow in 616, 625, and 626 Pumps by eliminating pulses and surges. Enable SILK for applications that are sensitive to flow rate and pressure and that use flow rates of 2.00 mL/min or less.

Notes:

  • Disable SILK when you perform flow-path maintenance or when you purge an autosampler. Always enable SILK for applications with flow rates less than 0.5 mL/min.
  • You cannot control the SILK control of the 625 using Empower software. You must use the 625 Controller's front panel to control the SILK.

Chart out

Type of analog data sent to the chart recorder through the 10-mV output on the rear panel of the instrument.

Choices: Off, %A, %B, %C, %D, Flow, Temperature (applies to the 600E, 616, 625 LC, and 626 Pumps only)

Default: %A
