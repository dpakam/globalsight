LS Calibration wizard - Normalization Standard page

Use this page to enter the information needed to normalize the different channels of light scattering data. This page contains different text depending on your selections on the Initial LS Calibration page. The table lists narrow standards that have the same instrument method name, system, and solvent as the current channel in Review.

If you selected Normalize with isotropic standard, then select at least one injection from the table before proceeding to the next page of the wizard. If you selected multiple standards, then normalization constants from the different standards are averaged to produce one constant per LS channel.

Once you click Next, the wizard starts the normalization calculation if you selected Normalize with isotropic standard.
