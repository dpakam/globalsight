Injector tab (6890 and 7673S)

Use this tab to specify operating parameters for controlling the front or back injector module on the 6890 GC system and the 7673S GC Autosampler.

Note: Not all functions are available to all injector models.

6890 instrument only functions

These functions are not controlled by Empower software. If your 6890 GC has these capabilities, you must control them directly from the instrument:

  • Waste bottle location
  • Slow plunger
  • Pre- and post-dwell time

Note: If you are using a 7673-style injector tower with a 6890 Series controller box, you might not be able to use all of the advanced injector functions on this tab, such as pre-washes or skim depth.

Solvent A or B pre-washes (6890 GC system only)

Number of times the syringe is rinsed with solvent from the solvent A or B bottle before an injection. The injector lowers the needle into solvent bottle A or B, fills the syringe to 80% of its full volume, and empties the syringe into one of the waste bottles.

Default: 0

Sample washes

Number of times the syringe is rinsed with sample before the injection. The injector lowers the needle into the sample vial, fills the syringe to 80% of its full volume, and empties the sample solution into one of the waste bottles.

Default: 0

Injector pumps

Number of times the syringe plunger moves up and down while the needle is in the sample. This up and down movement, which expels air bubbles to improve reproducibility, occurs before sample injection and after Sample Washes.

Default: 0

Viscosity

Number of seconds the plunger pauses at the top of the injection stroke. Increase the value of this parameter for viscous samples to allow time for the sample to flow into the vacuum created in the syringe.

Default: 3 seconds

Solvent A or B post-washes

Number of times the syringe is rinsed with solvent from the solvent A or B bottle after an injection. The injector lowers the needle into solvent bottle A or B, fills the syringe to 80% of its full volume, and empties the syringe into one of the waste bottles.

Default: 0

Note: To ensure that all sample lines experience the same treatment, post washes are performed before the first line in a sample set is run, just as they are run before subsequent lines in the sample set.

On column

Enables on-column injection with on-column inlets. When this feature is enabled, the injector changes the injection speed and lowers the tip of the syringe needle an additional ¾-inch (19 mm) into the column.

Choices: Enabled or Disabled

Default: Disabled

Caution: To avoid damaging the column by additional lowering of the needle, disable this parameter if the 6890 or 7890 GC System does not have an installed on-column inlet.

Skim enable (6890 system only)

Enables the Skim Depth (see below).

Choices: Enabled or Disabled

Default: Disabled

Skim depth (6890 GC systems only)

Adjusts the depth of the needle tip above or below its default position in the sample vial.

Range: -2.0 to 30.0 (a positive value raises the needle tip)

Default: 0.0. Units for Skim depth are 0.1 mm
