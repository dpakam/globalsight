New Sample Set Method wizard – Specify Calibration Options page

Use this page to select your method of calibration, using either standard vials that each contain a different concentration of standard, or a single standard vial from which the autoinjector makes injections of varying volumes. In addition, you define where your standard vials are located: in a group at the beginning of the tray, or before the start of each sample bracket. You define your calibration options from the selections described below.

Standard levels section

Use this section to define your calibration method.

Each standard vial contains a different level standard

Make this selection when you have a single standard vial for single-level calibration, or when you have multiple standard vials, each with a different concentration.

Different standard levels are created with different injection volumes using an Autoinjector

Make this selection when you use the autoinjector or autosampler to make injections of varying volumes from a standard vial.

Location of standards in tray section

Use this section to define where your standard vials are located in the tray. If you chose Dissolution method type, you will not see this section. If you selected No standards or at start of sample set in the Select Locations of Standards page, these options will be disabled.

Use this section to define where your standard vials are located in the tray.

All standards are located at the beginning of the Autosampler tray

Make this selection when all your standard vials are located at the beginning of the tray (before your samples).

Separate groups of standard vials are located throughout the Autosampler tray

Make this selection when you have separate groups of standards positioned before each group of samples.

After making your selections, click Next to advance to the next wizard page.
