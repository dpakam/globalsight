Temperature tab (2410)

Use this tab to control temperature for the internal oven in the 2410 Detector, and for one or two optional column heaters connected to the rear of the detector.

Internal temp enable

Enables temperature control of the internal oven.

Choices: Enabled or Disabled

Default: Disabled

Internal temperature

Temperature in °C for the internal oven. A typical operating temperature for the internal oven is about 5 °C above ambient for room-temperature applications. For high-temperature applications, set the internal oven temperature 3 to 5 °C below column temperature (depending on the length of tubing between the column heater and the detector. Internal Temp Enable must be selected for this setting to take effect.

Default: 30.0 °C

External temp 1 enable

Enables temperature control of external column heater 1.

Choices: Enabled or Disabled

Default: Disabled

External temp 1

Temperature in °C for external column heater 1. External Temp 1 Enable must be selected for this setting to take effect.

Default: 0.0 °C

External temp 2 enable

Enables temperature control of external column heater 2.

Choices: Enabled or Disabled

Default: Disabled

External temp 2

Temperature in °C for external column heater 2. External Temp 2 Enable must be selected for this setting to take effect.

Default: 0.0 °C
