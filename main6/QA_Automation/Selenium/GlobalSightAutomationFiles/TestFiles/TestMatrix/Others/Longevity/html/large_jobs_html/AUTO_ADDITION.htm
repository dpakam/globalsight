Editing Auto Additions

The Auto-Additions Editor gives you the ability to enter and edit the vial and injection volume entries and the delay time in Run Samples.

To enter or edit an entry:

  1. In the Auto Additions column, right-click the cell that needs entries or changes, then select Edit Auto Additions.

Result: The Auto-Additions Editor appears.

  1. If you want to enter a value, click an empty cell in the Vial column. Vial 1 and an injection volume of 1.0 µL are entered.

Tip: If you want to change a value, select a vial number in the Vial column or an injection volume in the Inj Vol (µL) column, then enter the new value.

  1. Repeat step 2 for each automatic addition you require.
  2. If you want to allow some mixing of sample in the needle, enter a new delay time (min).

Valid entries: 0.00 to 999.99 min.

Default: 0.00 min.

  1. Click OK.
