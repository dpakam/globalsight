General tab (7673S)

Use this tab to enable the front and/or back injector for the 7673 GC Autosampler.

Injector A enable

Enables the front autoinjector on the 7673 GC Autosampler.

Choices: Enabled or Disabled

Default: Disabled

Injector B enable

Enables the back autoinjector on the 7673 GC Autosampler.

Choices: Enabled or Disabled

Default: Disabled
