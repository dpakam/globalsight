Method Set wizard – Specify Extraction Type page

Use this page to specify the type of PDA or MS derived channel to create for your application. Choices:

  • Single Wavelength (PDA) – Created by extracting a single wavelength. The software prompts you for the wavelength.
  • Single Mass (MS) – Created by extracting a single mass. The software prompts you for the mass value.
  • Max Plot (PDA and MS) – Creates a chromatogram in which each data point is plotted at its maximum absorbance wavelength (PDA) or maximum intensity (MS). The software prompts you for starting and ending wavelengths (PDA) or masses (MS).
  • Total Plot (PDA and MS) – Averages absorbance values of a specific range of wavelengths (PDA) or sums intensities of a specific range of masses (MS). The software prompts you for starting and ending wavelengths (PDA) or masses (MS).
  • Ratio Plot (PDA and MS) – Represents a ratio of the absorbance at two wavelengths (PDA) or of the intensity at two masses (MS). The software prompts you for starting and ending wavelengths (PDA) or masses (MS), as well as the minimum value.
  • Total Ion Chromatogram (MS) – Where each point in the y-axis represents the sum of all ion intensities acquired in the spectrum at the time indicated by the x-axis.

After making your selection, click Next.
